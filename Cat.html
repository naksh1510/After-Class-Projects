Facts About Cats

Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world. They are known for their independence, playful nature, and ability to purr. Cats have been domesticated for thousands of years and come in a variety of breeds, each with its own unique characteristics. They are skilled hunters and can be found in various environments around the world. Cats communicate through body language, vocalizations, and scent marking. They are also known for their grooming habits, spending a significant amount of time cleaning themselves. Overall, cats make wonderful companions and bring joy to many households.

A study was conducted on cats being away from their owners for varying hours and the studies show that cats who were away from their owners the longest showed the greatest amount of affection!
